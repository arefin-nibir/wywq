Dr. Mohammad Haidar

A visionary leader in quantum technologies, driving innovation in computing, chemistry, and metrology through groundbreaking research and global collaboration.

Career Timeline View Expertise

Senior Quantum Scientist & Innovator

Dr. Mohammad Haidar holds a PhD in Physics from the University of Strasbourg (2012) and completed his postdoctoral research at Sorbonne University (2021-2023). As Founder and Lead of OpenVQA, and Senior Researcher at Qubit Pharmaceuticals, he mentors Master's students and spearheads projects in quantum chemistry and NISQ devices.

With over 325 citations, his work bridges theory and application in quantum software and metrology, establishing him as a global leader in the quantum revolution.
